<h2>category_profiles</h2>
<i><b>Get list of profiles in given category</b></i><br/>
Return structured array of profiles for given category<br/>
<i>$profiles = category_profiles(category)<i><br/>
Structured List on success, false on failure. 

<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
<pre>
$profiles = category_profiles("ATM");

</pre>
