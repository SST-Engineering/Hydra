<h2>text_processor</h2>
<i><b>Returns a record structure</b></i><br/>
$result = text_processor(input_target, def_file)<br/>
$result = text_processor(input_target, def_file, all_file_fields)<br/>
<i>Returns false on failure, array record on success</i><br/>
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
<pre>
   $def_file = "proforma_input.def";
   $record = text_processor($s, $def_file);
</pre>
