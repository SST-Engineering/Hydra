<h2>odbc_connect</h2>
<i><b>Connect to ODBC Server</b></i><br/>
$result = odbc_connect(connection name) .. or ..<br/>
$result = odbc_connect(dsn, user, password)<br/>
<i>Returns false on failure, true on success</i><br/>
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$conn = odbc_connect("KPI_Data_Connect");<br/>
$conn = odbc_connect("dsn", "TSJones","password");<br/>
<i>if $conn is not true begin<br/>
   console("Fails to connect"); exit;<br/>
   end<br/></i>
