<h2>excel_restrict_sheet</h2>
<i><b>Restrict the load to this sheet only</b></i><br/>
$result = excel_restrict_sheet(<i>sheet name</i>)<br/>
<i>Returns false on failure, true on success</i><br/>
Only load data from this sheet<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$result = excel_restrict_sheet("Sheet1");<br/>
