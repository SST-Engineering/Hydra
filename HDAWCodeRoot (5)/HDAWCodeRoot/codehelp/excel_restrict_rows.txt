<h2>excel_restrict_rows</h2>
<i><b>Restrict the load to these rows only</b></i><br/>
$result = excel_restrict_rows(<i>start_row</i>, <i>end_row</i>)<br/>
<i>Returns false on failure, true on success</i><br/>
Only load data from these rows<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$result = excel_restrict_rows(0, 6000);<br/>
