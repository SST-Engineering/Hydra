<h2>read_checksum</h2>
<i><b>Read a recorded checksum from ALCW table</b></i><br/>
$result = read_checksum(<i>your_reference</i>)<br/>
<i>Returns false on failure, the crc32 integer on success </i><br/>
The checksum is fetched with the profile or process identifier and <i>your ref</i> as key
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$crc32 = read_checksum("My Chk Ref 001");
