<h2>excel_restrict_columns</h2>
<i><b>Restrict the load to these columns only</b></i><br/>
$result = excel_restrict_columns(<i>start_column</i>, <i>end_column</i>)<br/>
<i>Returns false on failure, true on success</i><br/>
Only load data from these columns<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$result = excel_restrict_columns("A", "P");<br/>
