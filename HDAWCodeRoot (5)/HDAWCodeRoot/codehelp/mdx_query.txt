<h2>mdx_query</h2>
<i><b>Run an olap query to analysis services</b></i><br/>
$result = mdx_query(connection_string | connection_id, mdx_query)<br/>
<i>Returns the structured result on success, false otherwise</i>
<i>Example</i><br/>
<pre>
$result = mdx_query("ALC_OLAP", "SELECT {[Store]} ON COLUMNS, {[Account]} ON ROWS FROM [Budget]");
dump($result);
</pre>
