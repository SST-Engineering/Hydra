<h2>profile_category</h2>
<i><b>Return category of currently running profile</b></i><br/>
Get the category of this profile<br/>
<i>$category = profile_category()<i><br/>
Category string on success, false on failure
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
<pre>
$category = profile_category();
dump($category);
Might give "MI"

</pre>
