<h2>mdx_output</h2>
<i><b>Return the stdout and stderr streams after an mdx_query call</b></i><br/>
$result = mdx_output()<br/>
<i>Returns the strings of stdout and stderr after mdx_query, false otherwise</i>
<i>Example</i><br/>
<pre>
$result = mdx_query("ALC_OLAP", "SELECT {[Store]} ON COLUMNS, {[Account]} ON ROWS FROM [Budget]");
console(mdx_output());
</pre>
