<h2>excel_load</h2>
<i><b>Load the data from opened excel file</b></i><br/>
$result = excel_load()<br/>
<i>Returns false on failure, true on success</i><br/>
Load the data from the opened excel file (see excel_open) after restricting the data with
excel_restrict_sheet and excel_restrict_rows etc<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$result = excel_load();<br/>
