<h2>send_error</h2>
<i><b>Send an email to the error receiver</b></i><br/>
send_error("This is very serious");
<i>Send an error message as email to the nominated receiver of error mails</i><br/>
BE VERY CAREFUL. This should only ever be used in the most exceptional cases. It sends an email directly, it will stall the execution until sent.
It sends the message to the nominated receiver, who normally only expects serious system type errors.
