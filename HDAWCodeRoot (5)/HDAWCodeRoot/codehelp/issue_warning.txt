<h2>issue_warning</h2>
<i><b>Issue a warning message for this profile</b></i><br/>
Place a warning message associated with this running profile into the log<br/>
<i>$ok = issue_report("This profile had a db error")<i><br/>
True on success, false on failure, will place warning into the notes for this profile, into the log, and email users as appropriate.
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
<pre>
$ok = issue_warning($msg);

</pre>
