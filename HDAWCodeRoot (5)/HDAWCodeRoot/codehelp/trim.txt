<h2>trim</h2>
<i><b>Removes whitespace or other characters start of and end of target string</b></i><br/>
$result = trim(<i>target_string</i>)&nbsp;..or..<br/>
$result = trim(<i>target_string</i>, <i>characters_to_trim</i>)<br/>
<i>Returns the resulting string, false for failure</i><br/>
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$a = trim("   Hello World   "); // returns just Hello World<br/>
$a = trim("hello,.", ".,"); // returns just hello <br/>
