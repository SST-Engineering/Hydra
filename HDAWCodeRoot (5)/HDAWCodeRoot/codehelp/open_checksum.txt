<h2>open_checksum</h2>
<i><b>Begin to accumulate a checksum</b></i><br/>
$result = open_checksum()&nbsp;..or..<br/>
$result = open_checksum(<i>target</i>)<br/>
<i>Returns true </i><br/>
With a target, checksum is initialized with this string
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
open_checksum();
open_checksum("Hello World");
