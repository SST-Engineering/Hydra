<h2>write_checksum</h2>
<i><b>Write the checksum into ALCW table</b></i><br/>
$result = write_checksum(<i>your_reference</i>)<br/>
<i>Returns false on failure, true on success </i><br/>
The checksum is recorded with the profile or process identifier and <i>your ref</i> to make a unique
identifier for later recall.
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$ok = write_checksum("My Chk Ref 001");
