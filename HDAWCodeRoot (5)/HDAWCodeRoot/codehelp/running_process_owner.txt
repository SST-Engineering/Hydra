<h2>running_process_owner</h2>
<i><b>Get the owner details of the running profile</b></i><br/>
$result = running_process_owner()<br/>
<i>Return structure on success, false on failure</i><br/>
Structure is <br>
$result.Owner // Full name of owner<br>
$result.Email // Email of owner<br>

<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$this_proc_owner = running_process_owner()<br/>
email_this($this_proc_owner.Email, "Hello", "Hello {$this_proc_owner.Owner}");<br/>
