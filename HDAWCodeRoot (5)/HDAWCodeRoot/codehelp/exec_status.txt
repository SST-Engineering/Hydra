<h2>exec_status</h2>
<i><b>Get exit status after an exec call</b></i><br/>
$result = exec_status()<br/>
<i>Returns the close status after an exec call (that code's exit status), false otherwise</i>
<i>Example</i><br/>
<pre>
$result = exec("dir c:\");
console("Ends with status "+exec_status());
</pre>
