<h2>validate</h2>
<i><b>Validate data for a field</b></i><br/>
$result = validate(<i>field_name</i>, <i>value</i>)<br/>
<i>Return true on success, false on failure</i><br/>
Lookup the validation for this field in current profile and check value of field is valid
<br/>
<i>Example</i><br/>
$ok = validate("DeptNo", 56)<br/>
